About Me

I'm an American expat living in the Jewish homeland. After graduating from NYU in 2017, I made aliyah, realizing a life-long dream of mine. Soon after, I received my MBA from Tel Aviv University. I'm blessed to have been born into an amazing family with 2 awesome parents and 4 siblings, some of whom have gifted me the most delicious nieces and nephews. Originally from Los Angeles, I currently live in the Tel Aviv area with close friends. During the day, I spend my time as an associate at Israel's premier venture fund, Aviv Growth Partners. During the night, I still spend my time as an associate at Israel's premier venture fund, Aviv Growth Partners, but also try to get in some hoops, enjoy Wacky Mac, and curb my Twitter addiction. I'm passionate about building great things that have a marked impact on community. An unrelenting questioner, I find myself always asking the question "Why?" and trying to peel back the layers of reason behind religion, politics, and business. But mostly I just like eating Wacky Mac. If you're visiting this site it means you're probably curious about me--and the feeling is mutual! Feel free to reach out at belurie_at_gmail.com and I'd love to meet you
